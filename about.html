Who we are?

We are MDR Studio, a creative studio that combines the power of photography and graphic design to present visual works that are storytelling, aesthetic, and meaningful.

Since our founding in 2025, we have one goal: to help brands & individuals tell their stories through high-quality visuals. We believe that images are not just aesthetics, but vehicles of emotion, identity, and powerful messages that touch the heart and build real connections with the audience.

We offer professional graphy ranging from personal portraits, products, to event documentation, as well as communicative graphic design of visual identity, posters, social media assets, to print & digital marketing materials. Every project is carried out with an integrated approach, you tell your vision, we realize it visually and strategically.
